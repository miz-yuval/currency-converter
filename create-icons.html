Icon Generator

This utility generates the three placeholder PNG icons required by the Currency Converter extension. Click the button below — three files will download automatically. Move them into the icons/ folder.

Instructions:
1. Click the button above.
2. Move icon16.png, icon48.png, and icon128.png from your Downloads folder into the icons/ sub-folder of the extension.
3. You can then delete this file — it is not part of the extension itself.
